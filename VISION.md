# AiOS — AI Life Operating System

## Vision

AiOS is not a planner.

AiOS is a Life Operating System.

It continuously understands my life from multiple sources and automatically plans, tracks and adapts everything.

Instead of me managing tasks, calendars, repositories, emails and notes separately,

AiOS should understand them as one connected graph.

Every object in my life becomes connected.

Examples

Hackathon
↓
Repository
↓
Emails
↓
Team
↓
Meetings
↓
Calendar
↓

↓
Progress
↓
Notes
↓
Deadlines

The AI should always know:

• What I'm working on
• Why I'm working on it
• What's blocking me
• What's most important
• What should happen next

The system is local-first.

Cloud services are used only for syncing data.

All reasoning happens locally through Ollama whenever possible.
